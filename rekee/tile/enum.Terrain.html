pub enum Terrain {
    None,
    Asphalt(u8),
    Gravel(u8),
    Snow(u8),
}

Tile terrain information. Describes the track surface of a tile together with the danger level.

The meaning of the danger level value is:

  • 0: tile has no danger level assigned
  • 1: tile has danger level ‚Äúyellow‚Äù (low)
  • 2: tile has danger level ‚Äúorange‚Äù (medium)
  • 3: tile has danger level ‚Äúred‚Äù (high)

Use TileInfo::terrain() to get the terrain data of a specific tile.
